FAQ

How many team members can I invite?

You can invite upto 10 team members in one go!

What is the maximum file upload size?

No more than 2GB. All files in your account must fit your allotted storage space.

How do I reset my password?

You can simply click on the "Forget Password" button and follow the steps from there on.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription from "Your Profile" section at the top right corner.

Do you provide additional support?

Yes, we provide additional support. You can find out more by clicking "Features".
